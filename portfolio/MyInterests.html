American Sign Language

Did you know there are 3 types of signing in the U.S? Each English-speaking countries has its own signing? Those were first things I learned in ASL class and almost all students were surprised like I did. I started to learn ASL when my son was diagnosed with permanent hearing loss. My son has developmental delay too. But just like any other language, children pick up the new language much faster than adults. My son knows a lot more than I do. I enjoy learning his day from him and not only from his teacher.
